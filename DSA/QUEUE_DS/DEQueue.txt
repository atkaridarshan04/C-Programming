DEQueue - Double Ended Queue

DEQueue does not follow FIFO

We can do insertion and deletion from both sides in this. (if space is there)

Two Types of DEQueue
1.Input Restricted DEQueue :- In this insertion form front is not allowed.
2.Output Restricted DEQueue :- In this deletion from rear is not allowed.
